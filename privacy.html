Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Piggy Bong ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our K-pop fan spending tracker mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Spending Data: Purchase amounts, categories (concerts, albums, merchandise), dates, and notes you add
  • Budget Information: Budget limits and financial goals you set
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details and preferences
  • Support Communications: Messages you send to our support team

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, features accessed, session duration
  • Technical Data: IP address, app version, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Authentication Data: Login timestamps and authentication tokens

2.3 Third-Party Service Data

We use Supabase as our backend service provider, which may collect additional technical information for service provision and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain the Piggy Bong app functionality
  • Enable spending tracking and budget management features
  • Synchronize your data across devices
  • Process and store your financial tracking data

3.2 Communication

  • Send important app updates and security notices
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Provide customer service assistance

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Access and Control

  • Access: View and download your personal data through the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format

How to Exercise Your Rights:

Contact us at hello@piggybong.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days and may require identity verification for security purposes.

6. Data Security and Protection

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Authentication: Secure authentication mechanisms protect your account
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates

6.2 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Spending Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Data: Retained for 3 years for customer service purposes

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important: Piggy Bong is designed for users 13 years and older. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7.1 Age Restrictions

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Age verification is required during account creation

7.2 Parental Controls

  • Parents can contact us to review their child's information (ages 13-17)
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's account
  • We provide educational resources about financial responsibility

11. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email

hello@piggybong.com

Privacy Requests

Please include "Privacy Request" in the subject line

App Name

Piggy Bong

Response Time

We aim to respond within 48–72 hours

Effective Date: This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 29, 2025.

Thank you for using Piggy Bong! We're committed to protecting your data while helping you track your K-pop spending.
